# Create Reviewed Notion WBR

Convert the supplied Amazon notes into a final WBR record for the Notion Decision & Report Log.

Required output:

- Headline
- KPI vs target and prior period
- Validated drivers
- Risks
- Actions with owner and due date
- Decision needed
- Sources

Separate facts from hypotheses. Never invent metrics, owners, dates, targets, or sources. Use `[MISSING]` and `[VERIFY]` for unresolved inputs. The record is not ready for Notion until all markers have been resolved.
